Exchange Warranty Extension

Exchange Warranty Extensions start the day after the standard warranty expires. Brother will ship a factory-reconditioned unit the next business day to replace the customer's malfunctioning unit.

Onsite Warranty Extension

An Onsite Warranty will allow for remedial service at your location to restore the product to good operating condition under normal usage at no charge for parts or labor.

Depot Warranty Extension

Depot Warranty Extension allows for remedial service at a Brother Authorized Service Center to restore the product to good operating condition under normal usage at no charge for parts or labor.
