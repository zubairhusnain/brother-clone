Frequently Asked Questions

Is Artspira free?

Yes! There is a free version and a subscription version. Choose your own adventure!
What file types can I upload?

You can upload the following file types: (EMBROIDERY) PES, PHC, PHX, DST (CUTTING) FCM, SVG (PRINTING) JPEG, PNG.
On what devices can I download Artspira?

Mobile or tablet, iOS, and Android operating systems
How many machines can I connect to Artspira?

You can connect up to four machines in Artspira, it can be four of the same machines OR four different machines if they are all wirelessly enabled or Bluetooth compatible.
